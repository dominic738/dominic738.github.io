I love the Hunger Games franchise. I love it so much that I'm doing a whole website on it. But if I had to choose the best movie in the series, it would be the first one, The Hunger Games. THG Cathing Fire and both of the THG Mockingjay were good, but not as great as the first movie. The reason why I like the first movie better than all the others was because it really showed you the protagonists, the storyline, and how the games work. It told us about careers (people who spend their whole life training for the Hunger Games), all the districts *SPOILER ALERT* except for district 13, and all the rules of the games.
